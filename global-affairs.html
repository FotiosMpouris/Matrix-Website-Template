Global Affairs & Diplomacy

A Vision for American Leadership

Strengthening America's influence abroad is a crucial part of Making America Great & Healthy Again. Our Global Affairs initiatives prioritize fair trade, strategic partnerships, and open dialogue with allies around the world.

We aim to foster peace through strength, nurture diplomatic relationships, and champion freedom worldwide, creating an international environment where American ideals and prosperity can flourish.
