Project DOGE

Department of Government Efficiency

The Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) represents a major leap in rethinking how the federal government manages resources. Headed by a team of forward-thinking experts, including Elon Musk, DOGE is an attempt to merge private-sector innovation with public-sector accountability.
